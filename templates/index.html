Solver

Type a guess, then tap a tile — or press 1/2/3 — to cycle its color to match what Wordle showed you.

Arrow keys move between tiles; Enter submits and narrows the word list. The × on a row undoes that guess.

Practice

The app picks a secret word — six tries, colors fill in automatically.

Stuck? Try Hint, or play a specific day's puzzle from the calendar.
